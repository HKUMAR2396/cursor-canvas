Google App Campaigns: The Portfolio Approach

Success is collaborating with Google's AI by providing a diverse portfolio of creative assets.

Asset-Driven System

Provide the maximum inputs: 5 headlines, 5 descriptions, 20 images, and 20 videos. The algorithm finds the winning combinations.

Structured Testing

Use thematic asset groups (e.g., one for features, another for testimonials) to gain clearer insights into which messages resonate.

Leveraging Google's AI Tools

Use Google's suite of AI tools to automatically generate new video ads from store assets or intelligently trim longer content into multiple 6-second ads.

The Modern Growth Team
